View Pay Profile History

The pay profile history shows history of changes to the pay profiles along with the details which includes User, reason for change, IP address, Date time stamp etc.

 

To View Pay Profile History:

 

  1. Click on the Payroll Administration module and then select Pay Profiles option.

 

 

  1. Click on view to display the Pay Profile History.

The screen displays the list of existing pay profiles for the user to view.

 

 

  1. Click on the desired profile record to view the details of that profile.

 

 

  1. Click on any of the pay profile history record to view its details.
