View Pay Rule History

The pay rule history shows history of changes to the pay rules along with the details which includes User, reason for change, IP address, Date time stamp etc.

 

To View Pay rule History of a pay rule:

 

1.     Click on the Payroll Administration module and then select Pay Rules option.

A list of Pay rules gets displayed.

 

 

2.    Against any of the desired pay rule, click the View button to view history of that pay rule.

 

 

3.     Now, click on any of the desired history record to view details of that pay rule history.

 

 

4.     The details also show you the shift and pay conditions of the pay rule.

 

       The history can be exported in excel/spreadsheet format.
