View Pay Roll History

You can view the history of a payroll which shows information such as when and by whom it was created, edited, reason for change, IP address of the person, date and time stamp.

 

To view the History of a Payroll:

 

  1. Click on the Payroll module and then select PayRoll option.

 

    

 

    The list of existing payrolls are displayed as a list.

 

    

 

  1. Click View against any payroll, you will be taken to the payroll details of the selected payroll.

 

    

 

      The history can be exported in excel/spreadsheet format.

 

  1. You can again click on the payroll history displayed to again view the detailed history.
