Add Employee Annual Leave Entitlement

The administrator can set the maximum annual Leave entitlement for an employee based on their length of service which facilitates accurate processing of payment for the employees.

 

Considering the shifts worked, and absences, and the leave accrual rate, the system will calculate the Annual Leave accrual increment till that day. This allows the administrator to know the Annual Leaves accrued by the employee for a year.

 

To add employee annual leave entitlement:

 

  1. Click icon and then select the People menu.

 

 

A list of employee records gets displayed on a screen.

 

  1. Against an employee record, click .

 

The View Employee screen with Employee Info tab is displayed by default as shown below.

 

 

  1. Click the Leaves tab on the View Employee screen. The following screen gets displayed as shown:

 

 

  1. Inside this tab, click the Annual Leave tab. The following screen gets displayed

 

 

  1. Next, click on the Create New Leave button. The fields for accepting the annual leave parameters gets displayed:

 

 

 

Field

Description

Entitlement Name

Name of the leave entitlement

Service Days

Number of days of service being considered

Maximum Paid Leaves

Maximum paid leaves entitled for the employee

Applicable

Whether the leave entitlement is applicable or not

Current Maximum Annual Leave (View only)

Current Maximum Annual Leave existing on the employee’s balance

Annual Leave Accrued Days (View only)

Annual Leave Accrued Days on the employee’s balance. The accrual rate is set in the Pay Profile Annual Leave Accrual Rate % field. Based on this rate, the annual leave accrued days will be calculated and the result displayed here.

 

  1. Add or update the details in the desired fields.

  2. Click the  icon next to the entered values to save the annual leave entitlement for that employee.

  3. Check mark the entitlement to make that applicable for the employee.

 

Buttons

Description

Click the delete icon to delete the entitlement

Click this icon or the  icon to cancel the adding of an annual leave entitlement.

 

 

When the employee has accrued the maximum amount of leave days allowed,

Then the system will not increment or accrue any further annual Leaves for that employee.

 

The count of accrual leaves shall be reset to 0 balance on the calendar year end i.e. 31st December and new accrual will be started from the next day.
