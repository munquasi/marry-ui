Add Annual Leave

To add / apply Annual leave, click on the Annual Leave tab present on the Country Absence Entitlement screen. On clicking  button, the user can add new annual leave by providing the inputs for the fields present under Add New Leave tab as shown below:

 

 

 

The description of fields present on the Country Absence Entitlement screen > Annual Leave tab are given below:

 

Field

Description

Entitlement Name*

There are set of rules which are predefined and can be marked as  applicable/inapplicable depending on the designation of the employee.

 

On the Entitlement Name field, mention the name of rules entitled for that particular employee depending on the assigned designation.

Service Days*

The Service days are calculated on the basis of Date of Joining of the employee till Current Date of employeeâ€™s services in the organization.

 

On the Service Day field, mention number of days spent by the employee in service.

Maximum Paid Leaves*

The maximum number of paid leaves are the leaves that is taken off of work with pay (user will still be receiving salary while are on leave). Mention the number of leaves which are paid depending on the designation of that particular employee.

Effective Date *

The trigger for the update of the maximum entitlement for an individual employee will be based on the 'Effective start date' received from HCM and interfaced to OPS.

 

Click on the  icon, the calendar to select date will  get displayed as shown below:

 

Once the user select the effective date, it will get displayed on the Effective Date field.

 

Expiry Date

The Expiry date is the date on which the rules will get expired.To mention the expiry date, the user has to click on the Active check box and mark it   'unchecked'. The 'Expiry Date' field will get enabled.

 

Click on the icon, the calendar to select date will  get displayed as shown below:

 

 

Once the user select the expiry date, it will get displayed on the Expiry Date field.

 

Active

The  checkbox is displayed as checked by default. If user mark it  'unchecked', then the 'Expiry Date' field will get enabled and the 'Expiry Date' will get mandatory to be added.

Once you provide inputs for all mandatory field click on the  icon to load/add the rule applicable for annual leave for that particular employee, depending on the designation of the employee in the organization.

On clicking the  icon the added details will get saved and user will be redirected back to the Country Absence Entitlement screen > Annual Leave tab to view the saved details.

Click on the  button to cancel the process of creating rule applicable to the annual leave for that particular employee. The rule will be discarded and user will be redirected back to the Country Absence Entitlement screen > Annual Leave tab.

 

 

The field marked with (*) are mandatory fields.

 

On clicking the  icon , the annual leave rule will get loaded successfully and will be displayed on the list displayed below the Annual Leave.
