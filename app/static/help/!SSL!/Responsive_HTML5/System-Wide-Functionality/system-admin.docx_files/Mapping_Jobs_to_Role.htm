Mapping Jobs to Role

Jobs are mastered in Oracle HCM and stored in the Employee module in OPS. In the Roles module, we utilise this reference data and display the list of Jobs in the Job Title to Role Mapping screen.

 

Here, the System Administrator can map a Job Title to a Role. This allows a User to inherit a Role automatically based on their Primary Job Title in HCM.

 

To map a job title to a Role:

 

  1. Click icon and then select the Job Title to Role Mapping menu.

 

 

  1. The following screen is displayed on clicking the Job Title to Role Mapping menu.

 

 

  1. Click the link against the job title that you want to assign a role. The record expands with the various fields that allows the logged in user to update the required information:

 

 

  1. From the Role drop down list, select the appropriate role you want to assign to the selected job profile.

 

 

  1. After selecting the role to be mapped to the job profile, click Update to save the mapping. The following confirmation dialog displays.

 

 

  1. Click Confirm. The role selected will be assigned to the job profile.
