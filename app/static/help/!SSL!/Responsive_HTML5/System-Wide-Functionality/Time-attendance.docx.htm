Ops-4-4.1Control Room Dashboard

The control room dashboard displays the list of employee shifts and current status as well as the total statistics for each status. These statuses are:

 

●      AT-RISK: 15 minutes before up until check in/out time

●      In Jeopardy: From check in/out time until 15 mins after

●      Overdue: From 15 mins after onward

●      Upcoming: Upcoming shifts and their status

Ops-4-4.5 IVR

Employee raises the duress using IVR, on calling the IVR the duress procedure will be recognized during any of the IVR options (1,2,3) regardless of whether  the employee is scheduled to make a start call, end call or check call or no call.

 

Duress will be listed at the top of the CRD until required actions (duress recording) are completed for duress. The current process of IVR is to select any of the option from 1, 2 and enter Employee ID & Site ID as required. When any of the input is received from IVR, the system shall take it as a 'Duress' and shall take it as a top priority situation ignoring all the other factors like check in/out etc.

 

IVR scenarios will get validated by Tolerance, defined under contract for Check IN/Check Out.  For more information, please refer this section Ops-2.2-2.2.13 Tolerance.
