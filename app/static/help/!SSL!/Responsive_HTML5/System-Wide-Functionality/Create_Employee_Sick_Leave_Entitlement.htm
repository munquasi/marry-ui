Create Employee Sick Leave Entitlement

The user can apply or override existing sick leave entitlement and increase the Maximum Sick Leave entitlement for an employee based on their length of service. It facilitates the accurate payment employees based on Sick Leave days to be taken.

 

 

To configure this:

 

  1. Click icon and then select the People menu.

 

 

 A list of employee records gets displayed on a screen.

 

  1. Against an employee record, click . The information of the person gets displayed.

 

  1. The View Employee screen with Employee Info tab is displayed by default as shown below.

 

 

  1. Click the Leave tab on the View Employee screen. In this tab,  the leaves Sick Leave tab for the selected employee gets listed along with relevant detail.

 

 

  1. Next, click on the Create New Leaves button. The fields for accepting the sick leave parameters gets displayed:
