Minimum Wages

The Minimum Wages tab reflects all the details about the wages given to the employees. The minimum wages depends on age of the employee. it displays the following information about a wage.

 

  • Start Age and End Age of the employee

  • Minimum Wages given to the employee in that specific age gap

  • Start date and end date of the wage.

 

Edit Old Wages

At any point of time the Minimum Wages can be edited under the country module.

 

To edit a Wage:

 

 

  1. Click  icon on the Javelin Home screen and then select the Minimum Wages option.

 

 

A screen will appear displaying all the minimum wages under the Country Module.

 

  1. Click on Edit to edit the selected Minimum Wage.

The following screen will appear to edit the selected Minimum Wage.

 

 

 

  1. Click on SAVE once everything is filled edited or CANCEL to cancel the Edit Wage Tab.

Delete Old Wages

At any point of time the Minimum Wages can be deleted under the country module.

 

To delete a Wage:

 

  1. Click  icon on the Javelin Home screen and then select the Minimum Wages option.

 

 

A screen will appear displaying all the minimum wages under the Country Module.

 

 

 

  1. Click on  Delete the delete the selected Minimum Wage.

The following pop up will appear on the screen.

 

 

  1. Click on Confirm to delete the selected Minimum Wage or Cancel to cancel it.
