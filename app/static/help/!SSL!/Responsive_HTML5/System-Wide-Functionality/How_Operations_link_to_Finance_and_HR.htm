How Operations link to Finance and HR

An organization is incomplete without the following:

●      Managers

●      Employees

●      Sales team

●      Customers

 

All these can be managed by 3 teams:

●      Human Resource Team or Human Capital Management (HCM)

●      Operations Team (OPS)

●      Finance Team or Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)

 

 

Following information can be fetched from the diagram shown above.

●      The information of employees is managed by OPS and HCM team.

●      The sales team deals with OPS only.

●      The information of customers is managed by OPS and ERP.

●      The managers deals with all three (OPS, HCM and ERP).

Ops-1.7-1.7.1 Operations and HR

The link between Operation and HR can be understood in following simple ways.

 

1.     The operation managers and the human resource managers come together in the issues related to administration, payroll and other matters.

 

2.     Operations explains the effects of human resource activities such as pay, training, communications and staffing.

 

3.     Operations can also commonly perform a number of human resources management functions, such as assigning tasks, setting employee priorities and determining staffing requirements.

 

HCM and OPS in G4S:

 

The connection between HR and Operations in G4S can be understood by the following points:

 

4.     Ops can trigger a requisition creation in TALEO against a job. Candidate will apply for the same and then process as an employee from TALEO to HCM. HCM provides OPS with details of new hires (employee operational data) in order to utilize the additional hires in scheduling.

 

5.     Absences which are captured in Ops need to be interfaced to HCM, as HCM will be the master of Absences. OPS works out the actual duration based on the work schedule, and update the record accordingly.

 

6.     HCM  provides OPS with updates to employee information and employee terminations.

 

7.     Payslips will be created by the external Payroll system. These will be interfaced as PDF to be stored as a document of record (DOR) against the employee in HCM. Whereas, information related to interfaces, Employee Profiles, Job Profiles, National ID, Citizenship and Driver’s License are sent to OPS.

 

8.     Working hours, holidays and leave details of all the employees gets recorded in OPS. Hence, OPS update HCM with these values.

 

9.     OPS creates G4S employee’s account for work where an employee qualifies for email services. This will generate a G4S email address and passes back to HCM to be stored as the employee's work email.

 

10.  Content type items (Skills and Licenses) and Absence module fields (absence entitlement and adjustments) that are captured and approved in HCM are interfaced to OPS.

 

11.  HR masters G4S Work structure and push this to OPS in order for employees to be assigned for their posts. This includes Jobs, Locations and Organisations.

 

12.  Non contract specific Pay Elements for schedulable employees is mastered in HCM. These are interfaced to OPS so that OPS can proportion these across projects and include in the employee's gross pay file.

Ops-1.7-1.7.2 Operations and Finance

The link between Operation and Finance can be understood in following simple ways.

 

1.     Finance and Operations departments play very important roles in maintaining a correct flow of money in an organisation. Finance departments provide financial information necessary for the continuous operations of a business. Whereas, the operations department runs day-to-day operations.

 

2.     Tracking the flow of  money is one of the main roles of a finance department in an organisation (including the billing and accounting). Finance managers needs to keep track of sales and spending and produce yearly and quarterly statements. Further, they prepare financial reports and help to forecast the company´s financial future. Whereas, the operation management generally developsinternal policies, procedures and manages the operations staff. Additionally, operations managers monitor the company's performance and efficiency to make improvements and address problems.

 

3.     Financial departments need to also regularly update managers of other departments within an organization about the financial state of the business. Here comes the OPS in the picture. Based on the income statements the budget of the company is calculated. Moreover, the finance department needs to help the organization secure necessary funding and distribute profits in the form of dividends. This is controlled by the operation managers.

 

ERP and OPS in G4S:

 

The connection between HR and Operations in G4S can be understood by the following points:

 

1.     Once an account is created in OPS with all the financial information, the information flows to Oracle. The finance department creates credit limits after fetching this information and send it to OPS.

 

2.     ERP gathers the billing Information and Purchase Order Information from the OPS and initiates the process of billing the customer and paying the employees.
