Country Break Management

The Country Break Management  tab talks about break rules that are defined for a country. It is applicable across all employees. It displays the following information about a break.

1.     Daily duty hours of the employee

2.     Break Time given to the employee

3.     Date from which the break management is Effective

4.     Date till which the break management is Effective

 

 

Create New Break

 

The break can be created on under the Break Management tab of the country module. This data is reflected in the rate card of the contract. The break management will be reflected in the Rate Card tab of the contract module.

 

To create a New Break:

  

  1. Click icon on the Javelin Home screen and then select the Break Management option.

 

 

 

The following screen will appear displaying all the break managements under the Country Module.

 

 

  1. Click on Add New Break to create a new break detail under the Country Module.

          The screen for creating a new break will appear on the screen.

 

 

The descriptions of the fields in Create New Break Screen are given below:

 

Field

Description

Duty hours

2.             Number of hours the employees have to work per day.

Number of duty hours in a day cannot exceed the maximum hours available in a day (24 hours).

Break Time

The time available everyday as break to the employees (in minutes).

 

Once a time is entered in this field box, that time will be the break limit till the time the selected break management is effective.

Effective from

The date from which the break management is effective.

Effective to

The last date till which the break management needs to be effective

 

  1. Click on SAVE once everything is filled correctly or CANCEL to cancel the create new break tab.

Edit Old Break

At any point of time the break management can be edited under the country module.

 

To edit a Break:

 

  1. Click  icon on the Javelin Home screen and then select the Break Management option.

 

 

The following screen will appear displaying all the break managements under the Country Module.

 

 

 

  1. Click on Edit to edit any break management.

The following screen will appear to edit the selected break management.

 

 

 

  1. Click on SAVE once everything is filled correctly or CANCEL to cancel the edit break tab.

 

Delete Old Break

There may be a requirement to delete an existing break management at any time under the country module.

 

To delete a Break:

 

  1. Click  icon on the Javelin Home screen and then select the Break Management option.

 

 

The following screen will appear displaying all the break managements under the Country Module.

 

 

  1. Click on Delete to delete the selected break management.

The following pop up will appear on the screen.

 

 

  1. Click on Confirm to delete the break management or Cancel to cancel it.
