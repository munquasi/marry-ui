Customer Retention

This report shows the retention information of customers for a period based on the filter criteria.

 

To access the Customer Retention Report:

  1. Go to Report Management and click on Contract Reports.

 

  1. Next, select Customer Retention Report from the Reports drop down list  and click Go.

 

  1. On the report filters that gets displayed, select or enter the desired filter criteria.

 

 

The Start date and End date should be within a monthâ€™s range. And the End date should not be a future date.

 

 

All fields marked with * are mandatory. Please do not leave these fields blank to successfully generate the report.

  1. You can scroll the report to the right and left if required.

 

Some of the notable columns displayed in this report are:

Report Column

Description

Country

Country to which work order belongs

Department

Department to which the work order belongs

Branch

Branch which the work order belongs

Service Type

Type of service provided to Customer

Contract No

Unique ID of Contract

Contracted Hours

No of Contracted hours at start of report period (GG)

Terminated Hours

No of terminated hours during report period. (FF)

Retention %

Percentage of hours retained during period

Annualised Contracted Hours

No of annualised Contracted hours at 1 year prior to report start date(JJ)

Annualised Terminated Hours

No of terminated annualised hours from 1 year prior to report start date (HH)

Annualised Retention %

Percentage of annualised hours retained

 

 

The report can be downloaded in csv format.

 

This report shows the information for a maximum period of one year.
