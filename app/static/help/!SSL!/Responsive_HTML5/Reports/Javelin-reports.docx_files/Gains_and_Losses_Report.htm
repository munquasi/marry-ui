Gains and Losses Report

This report shows the Real Organic growth information of the company for a particular period.

 

Organic growth is the process of business expansion by increased output, customer base expansion, or new product development.

 

To access the Gains and Losses Report:

  1. Go to Report Management and click on Contract Reports.

  1. Next, select Gains and Losses Report from the Reports drop down list  and click Go.

  1. On the report filters that gets displayed, select or enter the desired filter criteria.

 

The Start date and End date should be within a monthâ€™s range. And the End date should not be a future date.

The report can be downloaded in csv format.

 

Report Period: Refers to the period specified in the report parameters

Previous Period: Refers to the same duration of period immediately preceding the report period.

 

 

This report shows the Gains and losses for a maximum period of one year.
