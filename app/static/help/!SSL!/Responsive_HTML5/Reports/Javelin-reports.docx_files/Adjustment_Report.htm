Adjustment Report

The pay adjustment report displays the following analytical information:

●   Value of pay adjustments in month

●   Total wage cost of direct labour hours

 

Value of pay adjustments in month: It is the aggregate value of all manual pay adjustments made in the month for the staff who have had their pay manually adjusted in relation to prior period errors and staff who have received a manual adjustment to pay for the current month (Total monetary value of adjustments. It is not the net figure of under-payments against over-payments).

 

Total wage cost of direct labour hours: It is the direct wage cost of all paid hours including non-billable hours (leave, sickness, on-job-training, induction training etc) including all pay adjustments for the period. This excludes other direct labour costs such as social insurances,pensions etc.

 

Users can have a summary or detailed view of the report.

 

To access the Pay Adjustment Pay Report:

1.    Go to Report Management and click on Payroll Reports.

2.    Next, select Pay Adjustment Report from the Reports drop down list and click Go.

3.    On the report filters that gets displayed, select or enter the desired filter criteria.

 

All fields marked with * are mandatory. Please do not leave these fields blank to successfully generate the report.

 

The Start date and End date should be within a month’s range. And the End date should not be a future date.

 

 

4.    After selecting the filter criteria, click Show Report. The report will get displayed similar to the one shown below:
