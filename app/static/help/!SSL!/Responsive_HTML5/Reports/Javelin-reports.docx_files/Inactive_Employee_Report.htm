Inactive Employee Report

The Inactive Employee report shows the list of inactive employees along with information about the numbers of SO's who have not worked in the month, SO's scheduled in month, employees who received payments for non hours related allowances. Who was paid but not scheduled (not including salaried).

 

To access the Inactive Employee Report:

  1. Go to Report Management and click on Operation Reports.

 

 

  1. Next, select Inactive Employee Report from the Reports drop down list and click Go.

  1. On the report filters that gets displayed, select or enter the desired filter criteria.

 

All fields marked with * are mandatory. Please do not leave these fields blank to successfully generate the report.
