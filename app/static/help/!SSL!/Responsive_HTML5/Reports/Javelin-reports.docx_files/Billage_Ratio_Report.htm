Billage Ratio Report

This report allows an MI/BI Manager/Contract Manager/Operations Manager to access following information on the billing ratio between 2 months:

●     Total Hours Billed in a Month

●     Total Hours Paid in a Month

 

It includes all non-billable hours, such as leave, sickness, training.

 

To access the Billage Ratio Report:

  1. Go to Report Management and click on Contract Reports.

 

  1. Next, select Billage Ratio Report from the Reports drop down list  and click Go.

 

 

  1. On the report filters that gets displayed, select or enter the desired filter criteria.

 

The Start date and End date should be within a month’s range. And the End date should not be a future date.

 

 

All fields marked with * are mandatory. Please do not leave these fields blank to successfully generate the report.

 

  1. After selecting the filter criteria, click Show Report. The report will get displayed similar to the one shown below:

Some of the notable columns displayed in this report are:

Description

Amount paid to the employees scheduled for above calculated scheduled hours. *

Total Uncovered Revenue Hours

Number of Scheduled hours without employee assigned

Amount paid to employees scheduled for above calculated scheduled hours. Contract charge rate are used.*

Total Covered Revenue Hours

Total Covered Revenue Charge
