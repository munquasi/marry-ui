Gross Pay Report

Gross pay is the total amount of money Employees get before taxes or other deductions are subtracted from their salary. The gross income or pay is usually not the same as net pay especially if taxes and other benefits such as health insurance are taken into consideration.

 

The Gross pay report displays the salaries of employees before taxes or other deductions and benefits are subtracted from their salary.

 

To access the Gross Pay Report:

  1. Go to Report Management and click on Payroll Reports.

  1. Next, select Gross Pay Report from the Reports drop down list and click Go.

 

  1. On the report filters that gets displayed, select or enter the desired filter criteria.

 

All fields marked with * are mandatory. Please do not leave these fields blank to successfully generate the report.
