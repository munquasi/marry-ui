Overgrading Report

This report shows the scheduled employees whose pay rate is higher than the contracted pay rate.

 

To access the Gross Pay Report:

  1. Go to Report Management and click on Payroll Reports.

Next, select Overgrading Report from the Reports drop down list and click Go.

 

 

  1. On the report filters that gets displayed, select or enter the desired filter criteria.

 

All fields marked with * are mandatory. Please do not leave these fields blank to successfully generate the report.

 

The Start date and End date should be within a monthâ€™s range. And the End date should not be a future date.

Some of the notable columns displayed in this report are:

Report Column

Description

Country

Country to which work order belongs

Department

Department to which the work order belongs

Service Type

Product Code/Service line

Contract No

Unique ID of Contract

Work Order No

Unique ID of Work Order

Location No

Unique ID of Location

Post ID

Unique ID of Post

Scheduled Hours

Total Scheduled hours for the period

Rate Card Pay Rate

Pay rate as defined on the Contract Rate Card

Total Costed Pay

Total Pay based on Costed rates

Scheduled Over Graded Hours

Total number of scheduled hours in the period where the employee scheduled is being paid more than the cost pay rate on the Rate Card

Scheduled Over Graded Cost

Total cost of scheduled hours in the period where the empoloyee scheduled is being paid more than the costed pay rate on the Rate Card

Total Actual Pay

Total of actual pay of scheduled employees (hours multiplied by rate)

% cost of overgrading

Percentage cost of overgrading (Total Actual Pay / Total Costed Pay)

% Overgraded Hours

Percentage of overgraded hours provided (Scheduled Over graded hours / scheduled hours)
