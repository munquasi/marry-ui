Time and Attendance

It is an attendance report that displays the Time and attendance Data check in/ Checkout out information on the basis of Employee assignment(s) based on the selected country, department, location, work order etc. for a given date range.

 

To access the Pay Adjustment Pay Report:

  1. Go to Report Management and click on Payroll Reports.

 

 

  1. Next, select Pay Adjustment Report from the Reports drop down list and click Go.

 

 

  1. On the report filters that gets displayed, select or enter the desired filter criteria.

 

 

All fields marked with * are mandatory. Please do not leave these fields blank to successfully generate the report.

 

The Start date and End date should be within a monthâ€™s range. And the End date should not be a future date.
