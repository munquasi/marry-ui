Delivery of Contracted Hours

This report provides the ratio of total number of permanent hours and total number of contracted hours in a month.

●   Total permanent contracted hours: The total number of permanently contracted hours that the business had contracted to supply in the month. Any contract that runs for more than 3 months is considered permanent.

 

●   Temporary contract hours: The number of hours in a month which the business was contracted to provide on a temporary basis and includes any contract with less than 3 months duration.

 

To access the Hours Comparison Report:

  1. Go to Report Management and click on Contract Reports.

 

  1. Next, select Delivery of Contracted Hours Report from the Reports drop down list  and click Go.

  1. On the report filters that gets displayed, select or enter the desired filter criteria.

 

The Start date and End date should be within a month’s range. And the End date should not be a future date.

 

 

All fields marked with * are mandatory. Please do not leave these fields blank to successfully generate the report.

 

  1. After selecting the filter criteria, click Show Report. The report will get displayed similar to the one shown below:

Image will be updated

 

  1. You can scroll the report to the right and left if required.
