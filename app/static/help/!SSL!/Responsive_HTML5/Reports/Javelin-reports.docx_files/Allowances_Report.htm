Allowances Report

This report shows the allowances allocated or entitled to employees of the selected country (based on the report filters applied).

This report displays allowances data of employees based on various filters like country, department, date etc.

 

To access the Allowances Report:

  1. Go to Report Management and click on Payroll Reports.

 

  1. Next, select Allowances Report from the Reports drop down list  and click Go.

 

  1. On the report filters that gets displayed, select or enter the desired filter criteria.
