Establishment Report

The Establishment report displays information on the percentage of operational staff vacancies. From this report, the user can also get information about the direct labour head count required to deliver all contracted hours in the most efficient manner possible, without any staff exceeding local working time restrictions in the period, on the last day of the period.

 

It also shows the actual head count i.e Total number of staff employed within the period (M) Report selection period Head count of person hired for that work order (aspiration).

 

To access the Establishment Report:

  1. Go to Report Management and click on Operation Reports.

 

 

  1. Next, select Establishment Report from the Reports drop down list and click Go.

 

 

  1. On the report filters that gets displayed, select or enter the desired filter criteria.

 

All fields marked with * are mandatory. Please do not leave these fields blank to successfully generate the report.
