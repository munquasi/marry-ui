Hours Reconciliation Report

This report shows the total number of hours logged by employees from all time logging modules of Javelin in order to compare and reconcile differences in the time logged. This will help in reconciling or minimizing errors in the system. The report shows Total contracted hours, scheduled hours, delivered hours (actual), billed hours and paid hours and the difference between all of these.

 

To access the Hours Reconciliation Report:

1.    Go to Report Management and click on Contract Reports.

2.    Next, select Hours Reconciliation from the Reports drop down list  and click Go.

3.    On the report filters that gets displayed, select or enter the desired filter criteria.
