Billage Ratio Report

This report allows an MI/BI Manager/Contract Manager/Operations Manager to access following information on the billing ratio between 2 months

 

  • Total Hours Billed in Month

  • Total Hours Paid in Month

 

     

Note: It includes all non-billable hours, such as leave, sickness, training. Permission criteria is also mentioned in the google sheet attached.
