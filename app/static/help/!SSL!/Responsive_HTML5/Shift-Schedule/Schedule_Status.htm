Schedule Status

To view the Schedule Shift for the post, click on the Shifts tab present on the Navigation bar and select the Schedule Status option from the drop down menu as shown below:

 

 

On clicking Schedule Status option from the Shift drop down menu present on the Welcome screen of Javelin application, the following Schedule Status screen will appear:

 

 

 

For viewing Schedule Status for the posts, mention the name of the post in search list and click on the search option to find the record. The result will get displayed on the bottom of the screen as shown below:

 

 

On the Schedule status screen the user can only view the details of the shift scheduled for each posts. To schedule the shift for any particular post, the user has to go to contract and follow the complete process as mentioned on the Shift Structure for Post.
