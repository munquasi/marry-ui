Shift

The schedule module is the block to communicate and manage work expectations. The schedule will lock employees to a set of shifts covering a calendar week period which is configured at the company level. It includes scheduling of working hours, training, allocating shifts, absence and many more activities.

 

To manage the Unmanned Shifts, Employee Training, Scheduling, Splitting Shifts and many more activities, the user can click on the Quick Links present on the Welcome screen of the Javelin application.
