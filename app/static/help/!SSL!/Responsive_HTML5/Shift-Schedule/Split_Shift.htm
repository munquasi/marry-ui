Split Shift

The split shift feature allows the shift manager/authorised person to split a shift amongst more than one employee, thereby maintaining contracted hours, even when an employee leaves a shift early.

  1. For the schedule line displayed, enter the start time and end time allocated for the split shift and then click Add.
