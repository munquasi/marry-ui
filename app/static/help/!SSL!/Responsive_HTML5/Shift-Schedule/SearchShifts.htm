Search Shifts

To search and view the shifts, the user can click on the link listed below the Shift Management tab present under Quick Links or by selecting the Search Shifts option from the drop down menu viewed by clicking on the tab given on the Navigation bar. The Navigation bar is displayed on the left side of the Welcome screen of the Javelin application.
