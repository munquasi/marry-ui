Licenses

This tab displays the list of licenses that an employee (Security Guard) would require before being posted to the customer site. You can manage the licenses by adding,  making them mandatory, or deleting them under this tab.

 

To add or manage license:

 

  1. Click the License tab. The following screen displays:

To view how to navigate to this screen, click here.

 

  1. Click the  icon on the Javelin Home screen and then click Customers option.

 

  1. The following screen displays with the option to search for an existing customer. Search for any existing customer by typing the relevant keywords and clicking the  button.

 

  1. On the list of customers displayed, click the desired customer record (highlighted in red). The existing account(s) related to the customer is displayed on the right side of the screen as (highlighted in blue) below:

 

 

  1. Click on the account link highlighted in red (shown in the preceding figure). The details existing under the customer account just clicked is displayed in tabbed view.

 

  1. Click on Contract tab to display the contract screen with the list of existing contracts (if any) as shown.

 

 

  1. Click on any of the existing contracts to view the details of the contract.

 

 

 

  1. Click Edit on the Licenses screen. The following dialog displays.

      

  1. Click Continue on the dialog that appears, to proceed. The following screen displays:

 

  1. In the resulting screen, click on the License search bar and select the desired license as shown.

 

  1. Next, click the Add License skill button. The license will get added to the list of licenses required for the contract.

 

  1. Click the Save button to save the license in the list of skills required for the contract.

 

    • User can checkmark icon to make the skill mandatory for the contract.

    • Click the Close button to remove the license from the required list for the contract
