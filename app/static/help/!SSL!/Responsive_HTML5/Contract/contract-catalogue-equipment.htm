Equipment

In the Equipment tab you can keep the record of equipment used or about to be used by the employees on the contracted sites. To manage this you can add them as an inventory or an asset in the system.

 

Inventories are the equipment supplied by G4S whose ownership is passed from G4S to the Client.

Assets are equipment used and owned by G4S.

 

You can perform the following tasks from this screen:

  1. Add Inventory

  2. Add Asset

  3. Edit Equipment

    Billing for Equipment

G4S provides high level of security 24x7 to its Employees all over the world. Its very important to equip its employees with proper equipment required during their duty. This feature comes under the Equipment Tab of the Catalogue tab under the contract level. It involves Inventory and Assets.

 

Inventory is equipment supplied by G4S whose ownership is passed from G4S to the Client.

Assets are equipment used and owned by G4S.

 

In the Equipment tab the user can keep the record of equipment used or about to be used by the employees on the contracted sites. To manage this the user can add them as an inventory or an asset in the system.

 

There are two types of features in Asset Equipment.

1.     Asset one off  : In this billing is done only one time.

2.     Asset bill plan : In this billing is done depending upon the bill cycle.

 

Billing is done only one time in Inventory equipment (like Asset one off).

 

As soon as billing is processed in the OPS system, the events are sent into PPM (Project Portfolio Management). Then PPM schedules an automatic process to transfer the PPM invoice to Accounts Receivables. Then in Accounts Receivables there is a process scheduled to automatically import the invoice from PPM. Then invoice is sent to the customer.

 

The equipment tab consists of adding / editing Inventories and Assets.

 

Refer Add Inventory to know how to add an inventory and Add Asset to know how to add an Asset. Refer Edit Equipment to know how to edit them.
