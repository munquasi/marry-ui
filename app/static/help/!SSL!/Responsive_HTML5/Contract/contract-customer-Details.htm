Customer Details

You can view the details of the existing customers in the Customers section of the Contract Module.

 

To search for a Customer:

 

  1. Click  icon on the Javelin Home screen and then select the Customers option.

 

   

 

The following screen displays with the option to search a customer by name.

  1. Enter the name of the customer you want to find in the search bar, and click the  icon, or press Enter key, to proceed with the search.

You can also search the customer with initials of the customer.

A list of customers matching the search keywords (or letters) appears on the screen.

 

  1. Click on a customer record to view the accounts related to the selected customer.

 

 

  1. Next, click on the customer account link to view the full details of that customer account.

 

 

  1. The details of the customer account is displayed in tabbed view.

 

 

You can switch between the following tabs in the customer view.

  • Details

  • Sites

  • Contracts

  • Contacts
