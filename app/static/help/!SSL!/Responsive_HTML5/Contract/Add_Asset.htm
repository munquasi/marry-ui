Add Asset

Assets can be provided by the Customer or by G4S. This information is added in the Contract module. To get the benefits of  assets, it is important to add a new assets with the required fields. The assets can be added in a contract under the Catalogue tab of the contract details.

 

To add an Asset:

 

To view how to navigate to this screen, click here (click again to collapse).

 

  1. Click the  icon on the Javelin Home screen and then click Customers option.

 

  1. The following screen displays with the option to search for an existing customer. Search for any existing customer by typing the relevant keywords and clicking the  button.

 

  1. On the list of customers displayed, click the desired customer record (highlighted in red). The existing account(s) related to the customer is displayed on the right side of the screen as (highlighted in blue) below:

 

 

  1. Click on the account link highlighted in red (shown in the preceding figure). The details existing under the customer account just clicked is displayed in tabbed view.

 

  1. Click on Contract tab to display the contract screen with the list of existing contracts (if any) as shown.

 

 

  1. Click on any of the existing contracts to view the details of the contract.

 

 

  1. Click the Catalogue tab. The Catalogue screen will appear with the following tabs:

●      Rate Card

●      Equipment

●      Allowance

●      Direct Management.

By default the system will show Rate Card tab.

 

 

  1. Click on the Equipment tab.

 

The Add Inventory and Add Assets button would be displayed with the list of equipment added (if any).

 

 

  1. Click on the Add Asset button in the current tab to add a new asset.

The following screen will appear displaying all the information required for adding an asset.

 

    

 

    The descriptions of the fields on the Add Asset Screen are given below:

 

Field

Description

Name

Name of the equipment to be added as an asset.

Description

A significant description of the equipment kept as an asset.

Bill Plan

The bill plan with which the Inventory is associated.  Invoices will accordingly be generated as per the billing cycle and sent out to the customer for payments of the bill. This is defined in the Bill Plan tab under selected Contract. From the Bill Plan drop-down list select a suitable bill plan for the asset that need to be added.

Max Quantity Supplied

The maximum quantity of the asset that is supplied to the customer.

Start Date

Capture the start date for the Asset.

End Date

Capture the end date for the Asset.

Unit Charge

Charge per unit of the asset.

Service Code

A code for the equipment to which it needs to be linked.

Charge Method

The method by which the assets will be charged from the customer.

Select the required Charge method for the equipment from the Charge Method drop-down list.

Revenue Event

A record about the revenue details associated with the asset.

Invoice Event

A record about the invoice details associated with the asset.

 

  1. Click on Save once all the required details are entered. You can also click Cancel to cancel the process.
