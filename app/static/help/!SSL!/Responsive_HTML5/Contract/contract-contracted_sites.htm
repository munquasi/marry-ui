Contracted Sites are the sites associated to a particular customerâ€™s contract, user can select or add any of the sites to the contract to provide the desired services to the customer under that contract.

Add Contracted Sites

User can add contracted sites at the contract level for the customer, when there is an requirement raised by the customer for services at the desired site or location.

 

To add a Contracted Site:

 

  1. Click the Contracted Sites tab.
    The list of pre-added and defined sites for that customer will be displayed.

 

To view how to navigate to this screen, click here (click again to collapse).

  1. The following screen displays with the option to search for an existing customer. Search for any existing customer by typing the relevant keywords and clicking the  button.

 

  1. On the list of customers displayed, click the desired customer record (highlighted in red). The existing account(s) related to the customer is displayed on the right side of the screen as (highlighted in blue) below:

 

 

  1. Click on the account link highlighted in red (shown in the preceding figure). The details existing under the customer account just clicked is displayed in tabbed view.

 

  1. Click on Contract tab to display the contract screen with the list of existing contracts (if any) as shown.

 

 

  1. Click on any of the existing contracts to view the details of the contract.

 

 

 

 

 

User should select a contract whose status would be Draft to add a Contracted site.

 

  1. Under the Add a Contracted Site heading click the icon next to the site name in the list that you want to add to the selected customer contract. The list of site names will be fetched from the sites tab under customer view details.

 

 

 

In case of Active contract, an button will appear on this screen which user will need to click first to bring up the button.

 

  1. Either check-mark the temporary option (and fill the start date and end date) , or just click the Add button to add the contracted site to the list displayed in the list.

 

 

The selected site record will be moved to the pre-added site list above the Add a Contract Site heading in the same screen.

 

 

 

To remove the selected contracted site you can click on Remove button to remove the contracted site from the list.
