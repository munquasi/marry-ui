Customer Contacts

The user can create and manage contact information for a selected customer via the contact tab under customer view screen.

Add Contacts to Customer Record

A single customer can have multiple contacts, these contact details need to be added to the record for proper communication.

 

To add a Contact:

 

  1. Click the Contacts tab to add contacts for the customer.

 

 

  1. Next, click Add Contact button in the contacts screen to display the following screen.

 

 

  1. Enter the contact details of the customer.

  1. Click the Contact Point button to enter a contact point for the customer. The following screen displays to include a contact point.

 

  1. Select the type of contact point you want to give from the Contact Point Type drop down list.

  2. Fill in the various fields required for the contact point type you have selected.

  3. Click Save to proceed with the creation of the contact for the customer.

 

If you click on Save without entering a Contact Point then it will show a message prompting you to enter a contact point.

 

The saved contact will appear in the list of contact under the Contacts tab of the customer.

Edit Contacts of customer record

If any customer contact information is been changed, in that case customer contact needs to be updated.

 

To edit a Contact:

 

  1. Click the Contacts tab to add contacts for the customer.

 

 

  1. Click any of the contact that needs to be updated. The Contact Details screen will open with existing pre-populated data.

  1. Click on Edit to edit the details. The fields on the screen become editable.

In the edit mode screen, the user can:

  • Edit a contact in the contact section

  • Add or edit contact point in the Contact point section

  • Add or edit Contact Responsibility in the Contact Responsibility section.

 

  1. Once all the fields are edited, click on SAVE.
    The selected contact will get updated and user will be navigated back to the previous page.
