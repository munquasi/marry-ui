Customer

The Customer feature allows users to view, capture  and manage the list of customers of G4S so that users can create contracts related information for the customer .

 

While creating a customer the system captures the basic yet important information related to the customer i.e. Billing Address, Contact Person details, site detail and contract detail etc. The customer creation screen also captures the details of the account manager who will, in future, be in charge of the account for the customer.
