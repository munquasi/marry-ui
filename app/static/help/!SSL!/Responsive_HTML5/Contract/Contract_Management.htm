Contract Management

The Contract management module allows the user to manage contracts from vendors, partners, customers, or employees. It supports the entire customer and contract life cycle which covers any process that contributes, creates or utilizes contract data. Contract management is seamlessly integrated into the process for tracking and expediting all contracted related workflows.

 

The Contract Management module provides the following inter-related sub-modules so that users can create contracts related information for the customer.

  • Customer

  • Contract
