Site Administration

When the user creates a customer account under  > Customer, a site is also be defined for that customer. This site detail is used to identify the location of the work orders and to facilitate various other customer related services.

 

 

The format and method of accepting the above mentioned site information for a customer account can be configured in the  > Contract > Site Administration page.

 

To configure how site information is accepted in the  > Customer > Sites,  user needs to perform these steps:

  1. Click on the  icon and then Contract menu as shown.

 

 

On clicking the Contracts menu, a list of recent contracts is displayed as shown below:

 

 

The screen shows a list of recent contracts along with their key details and various current status.

  1. Select the country for which you need to configure the site information input.
