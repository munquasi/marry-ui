Bill Plans

The bill plan section allows the contract manager to add and manage the billing plan for the customer of the particular contract. Invoices will accordingly be generated as per the billing cycle and sent out to the customer for payments of the bill. The creation of a bill plan allows the contract manager to  associate the New Bill Plan to Work Order. The contract Manager can add more than one Bill Plan to a Contract.

 

Any creation/modification done on the Billing Plan will be logged in the Audit Log.

 

Once a Billing Plan is saved/edited, changes will be sent to Oracle finance.

The descriptions of the fields in Bill Plans tab are as follows:

 

Field

Description

Method Name

Name of the method used for the billing

Billing currency Type

Type of billing currency use:

●      Contract

●      Transaction

●      User

Bill to Site

This is the site for which the bill will be generated. The default stipulated on the Contract will automatically appear here. However user can override this any specify any known Site associated to the Account.

First Bill Offset Days

The number of days that will be offset or not counted in the first bill.

Start Month

Month of the year when the billing will start to be considered in the invoice

Billing Cycle

Billing frequency or cycle which can be:

●      Daily

●      Fifteenth of month

●      Immediate

●      Weekly, and so on

Payment Terms

Specifies the period allowed to a customer to pay amount due, and may provide provision for cash in advance, cash on delivery, a deferred payment period of 30 days or more, or other similar provisions.

Billing Method

The billing method (whether variable or fixed)

Invoice Comments

Comments that will appear in the invoice sent to the customer.
