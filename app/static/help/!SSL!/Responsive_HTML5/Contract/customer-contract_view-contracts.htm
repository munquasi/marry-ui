Contract Details

When you view the details of an existing contract, you can see the following sections:

 

The descriptions of the fields in Contract Details section are as given below:

 

Field

Description

Account Name

This is the account name of the customer under which user will be creating a contract.

Status

Status of the contract during the creation. It can have the following status:

  • Draft status: A contract is in draft status until a contract manager collects the required level of details, and turns the status to Active at which point, it is interfaced to ERP.

  • Active status: An active contract can still be modified, and have contract lines (work orders) associated with it. It does not require any contract lines associated with it to be in active status.

Contract Number

It is the auto generated identifier for the contract using which the associated contract details will be tracked or updated or acted upon as necessary.

Contract Document Number

The document number attached to the contract

Contract Manager

The contract manager managing the contract. It will be pre-populated, as it is already been defined while creating the customer record.

Contract Type

The type of contract

  • Indefinite

  • Short Term

  • Fixed Term

Service Line

The type of service to be provided according to the agreement in this contract:

  • Cash Solution

  • Manned Security

  • Security system

  • Care and Justice service

  • Risk and Consultancy. and so on

LOI (Letter of Intent)

In some cases it may be the case that the contract papers are not ready and services are required by the customer immediately, in such cases services can be provided on the basis of Letter of Intent (LOI) signed by the Customer, this is nothing but a written confirmation from the Customer that he agrees to the terms and conditions of the contract. The LOI validity should not be more than a month. The contents of LOI is hidden

Sales Person

The Sales person responsible for the contract

Start Date

Start date of the contract

End Date

End date of the contract

Signed Date

Date the contract was signed

Currency Code

Currency code of the currency which is being used in the contract agreement. Billings and payments will be done in the currency denoted by the currency code.

Business Unit

The business unit responsible for providing service for this contract

Legal Entity

The legal entity that will be responsible for managing this contract

Organization

Organization associated with the contract

Legal Contract number.

Legal contract number for the contract

Original System

The original system for the contract

Primary Contract contact

The primary being used for the contract. It is set by viewing an existing customer account, clicking Contacts tab and then clicking Add contact point button.

Bill to Site

This is the site where the bills will be sent. The bill to site of an account is created in the Sites tab of the View customer screen under contract management.

Customer reference 1/2

References for the customer. Any invoice produced for this contract will have the Customer’s own references if provided

Description

Brief description of the contract or comments if any.

 

 

Please  note  that  after  a  contract  is  authorized,  the  “Signed  date”  and  the “ Start date” cannot be changed.

 

 

The descriptions of the fields in Contract Terms section are as given below:

Field

Description

Full Contract Term W.E.F

This is the commencement date the full contract term will come into force.

PO Required

Whether Purchase Order is required for this contract. 

G4S Termination period

Notice period that G4S will give to the customer while terminating the contract before the end of the contract period.

Customer Notice Period

Notice period that a customer can give to G4S while terminating the contract before the end of the contract period.

Deed or Seal

Whether any deed or seal is associated with this contract.

Limit of liability for direct loss

The liability or nonliability agreed to be paid in case of direct loss while fulfilling the contract . It can be:

  • No Liability

  • Specific amount

  • Contract value or

  • Unlimited liability

Limit of liability for indirect loss

The liability or nonliability agreed to be paid in case of indirect loss while fulfilling the contract . It can be:

  • No Liability

  • Specific amount

  • Contract value or

  • Unlimited liability

Liability currency

The agreed currency that will be used to settle in case any liability arises.

Nominal Contract value

Value of the contract without calculating for inflation. The customer need to have a Credit Limit which is greater than Nominal Contract Value.

 

 

To Activate a contract it MUST have at least one Bill Plan. It must also have at least one Revenue Plan.
