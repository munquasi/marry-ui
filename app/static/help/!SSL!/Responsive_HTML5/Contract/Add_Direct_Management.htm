  1. Click on the Direct Management tab. The following screen will appear.

 

         

 

  1.  Click on the Direct Management Button (as shown) to add the Direct Management details for the current contract.

The following screen will appear.

 

 

  1.  Fill the required details.

The descriptions of the fields on the Direct Management Summary screen are given below:

 

Field

Description

Bill plan

The Bill plan for which the employee is working. This is defined in the Bill Plan tab under selected Contract. This ensures that the charge entered can only be applied  for the given billing cycle.

Unit Charge

Unit charge of the direct management. This unit charge will be paid according to the billing cycle assigned in the bill plan.

Customer Reference

A reference number useful for getting track of the customer details for the assigned direct management,.

Service Detail

The work area of the employee.

Name

Name of the Direct Management.

Description

Briefly explain about the selected Direct Management.

Start Date

Captures the Start date for the Direct Management.

End Date

Captures the End date for the Direct Management.

 

 

The system will not allow to set up a direct management charge for a monthly billing cycle and apply it to a customer location (which is associated to a Work order with a quarterly billing cycle)

 

  1.  Click on Save once filled completely or click on cancel to delete any change.
